Method: The WorldMind Framework

WorldMind transforms autonomous agents into empirical learners. Instead of relying on resource-intensive training, it employs a Predict-Act-Verify loop to align the agent's internal world model with reality.

Figure 1: Conceptual Illustration of Experiential Alignment. The agent aligns its internal world model via Process Experience and Goal Experience.
Figure 2: Overview of the WorldMind Framework. The agent autonomously constructs a World Knowledge Repository (WKR) by unifying Process Experience (from prediction errors) and Goal Experience (from successful trajectories) to guide grounded simulation.

The core innovation lies in separating knowledge into two streams: Process Experience for physical boundaries (e.g., "cannot open a closed fridge without being close") and Goal Experience for high-level planning shortcuts.
