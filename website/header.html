Visualizing the Physical Hallucination Problem

Current LLM agents often understand the "why" but fail at the "how", leading to plans that are logically sound but physically impossible.

Without WorldMind

Goal: "Slice bread."

Action: Tries to slice without holding a knife.

Result: Physical Hallucination.

With WorldMind

Goal: "Slice bread."

Simulation: "Error: Need tool." ➜ Pick up knife.

Result: Success via Experience.
